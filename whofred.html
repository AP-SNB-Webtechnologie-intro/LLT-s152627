FRED AGAIN..

Frederick John Philip Gibson (born 19 July 1993), known professionally as Fred Again or simply Fred, is an English record producer, singer, songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and DJ.

Fred's rise in popularity seems to have stemmed from the release of 'Merea (we've lost dancing)', a song sampling the artist The Blessed Madonna talking about the importance of dancing and music during the pandemic. Not only speaking to the importance of music, the track includes messages of the vitality of friends.
